INSTALLATION AND SUPPORT

  1. System requirements:
    • Intel® Pentium® 4 or Intel Celeron® (or compatible) 2GHz processor (dual-core processors and those with Hyper-Threading Technology supported)
    • Microsoft® Windows® XP with Service Pack 2 or 3 or higher
    • 256MB of RAM
    • 50MB of available hard-disk space
    • Color monitor with 16-bit color video card
    • 1,024x768 monitor resolution at 96dpi or less
    • CD-ROM drive
  2. You must install Agragami with an account that has Administrative privileges (not a Limited User Account if you are using Vista).
  3. For the easiest installation experience, quit any open programs, including virus protection software, prior to installing Agragami.
  4. You do not necessarily need to uninstall previous versions of Agragami; some people prefer to keep the older, familiar version around as they come up to speed with the new version's features.
